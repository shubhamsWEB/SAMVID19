About SAMVID'19

We love our public

Samvid, SSTC Bhilai the largest cultural festival in Central India, present you yet another mind-boggling edition of its 3-day extravaganza. This time as you walk down the lanes of the campus, let’s together orchestrate the times we have lived through. And to help you make another one of those cherished memories, SSTC is ready and waiting for you, this 28th February to 2nd March.
Come and feel the fun. See you at Samvid ‘19!
#MachaateyRaho!

What SAMVID is All About?

A Nation Wide Techno-Cultural Fest which will encompass as one of the Holistic event ever happened in Chhattisgarh State, assimilating many cultural and technical events into one holistic event attracting a mass of nearly 13,000 students from all major colleges in Chhattisgarh and other states in vicinity. Empowering latent talents, to put there calibre at test in a National Platform.
